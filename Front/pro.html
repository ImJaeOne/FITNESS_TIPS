Overview of men’s weight training programs
  • Exercise details
  • Target muscle: Upper Pectoralis Major
  • Synergists: Anterior Deltoid, Triceps Brachii
  • Dynamic stabilizer: Biceps Brachii (short head only)
  • Mechanics: Compound
  • Force: Push
  • Starting position: Sit on an inclined bench with a dumbbell in one hand. Position the dumbbell to the side of your chest with your elbow bent and slightly tucked into your torso. Spread your feet and grasp the bench with your free hand for support.
